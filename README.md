# Capability Evidence Model (CEM)

**Status:** Early-stage research framework. In active development.

---

# What is CEM?

The Capability Evidence Model (CEM) is a framework for evaluating human capability through evidence rather than summaries.

Most evaluation processes—whether hiring, admissions, promotions, or choosing a collaborator—ask people to compress their capability into something small enough to be judged quickly: a CV, a portfolio, or a short interview.

That compression often obscures or omits important evidence of capability.

CEM explores a different question:

> **Can capability be investigated instead of summarized?**

Rather than evaluating descriptions of capability, CEM investigates the reasoning, evidence, and development behind that capability to support better-informed human decisions.

Hiring is the first application of CEM.

It is not its definition.

---

# Where CEM Came From

CEM did not begin as a hiring product or a research project.

It emerged while I was building a portfolio over fifty days alongside a full-time job.

As the work became deeper, more structured, and better documented, it became increasingly difficult to communicate through processes designed to evaluate people in only a few minutes.

That experience raised a broader question.

Perhaps the problem was not my ability to explain the work.

Perhaps the problem was expecting capability to be compressed into a summary in the first place.

CEM is the result of exploring that question.

---

# Current Status

CEM is an active research project.

The framework is still being developed, challenged, and refined.

Nothing is accepted because it sounds convincing.

Every significant conclusion must earn its place through reasoning, evidence, and critical review.

---

# What I'm Looking For

**Honest pushback, not validation.**

If you think an assumption is weak, a conclusion does not follow, or an idea does not hold together, I'd rather discover that now than after building more on top of it.

The purpose of this repository is not to defend CEM.

The purpose is to discover whether it survives careful scrutiny.

---

# Repository Philosophy

This repository documents both the framework and the process used to build it.

Every chapter answers one question.

Every conclusion shows its reasoning.

Every chapter can be challenged.

Every conclusion remains open to revision when better reasoning or stronger evidence emerges.

Nothing earns a permanent place simply because it was written first.
